Manchester Strada

Established in 2018, Manchester Strada Cycling Club is a new club to Manchester’s rich cycling scene. The club is aimed at all riders of all capabilities, whether you’re new to road cycling or a seasoned vet.

MSCC is social club with weekly club rides suitable for all, which will of course include a cafe stop or two. At its heart MSCC is not a competitive club but will happily support members should they wish to compete as MSCC.
